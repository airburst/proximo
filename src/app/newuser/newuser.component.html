Privacy Information

Your location is only shared with people whose invitations you accept, or who you invite (and they accept).

Your location is only updated if you visit this site (https://proximate.fairhursts.net).

This app only stores your name and your last location; it does not track where you have been.

Your location is only visible to your contacts if you visited the site within the last 24 hours.

You can stop sharing your location with contacts at any time.
